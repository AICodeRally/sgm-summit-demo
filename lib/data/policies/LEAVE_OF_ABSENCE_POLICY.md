# LEAVE OF ABSENCE & INACTIVE STATUS POLICY

**Policy Code:** SCP-005
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** APPROVED
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## PURPOSE

Define commission treatment during employee leave of absence (unpaid, medical, parental, sabbatical).

---

## LEAVE OF ABSENCE (LOA) TREATMENT

### During Leave Period

**Quota:** Pro-rated for period of leave
- Example: 1-month leave = quota reduced to ~92% (11 months of year)
- Longer leaves: Pro-rated accordingly

**Commission Eligible:** NO commission earned during LOA
- Rep not on active status
- No deals credited during leave
- Territory may be covered by another rep

**Draws:** Suspended during LOA (if applicable)
- Rep may have outstanding draw balance
- Repayment accelerated upon return or termination

---

### Return from Leave

**Ramp-Back Quota:** First 3 months after return
- Month 1 back: 80% of normal quota
- Month 2 back: 90% of normal quota
- Month 3 back: 100% of normal quota

**Territory Restoration:**
- Primary territory restored
- Any accounts covered during LOA transition period (30-60 days)
- Legacy revenue credit for transferred accounts

**Benefits Restoration:**
- Commission plan eligibility restored immediately
- Draw eligibility depends on performance, per case review

---

## TYPES OF APPROVED LEAVE

### Unpaid Leave

**Definition:** Employee-initiated leave without pay

**Commission Treatment:**
- No commission earned
- Quota pro-rated
- Draws not applicable during leave
- May take up to 30 days unpaid leave per calendar year

### Medical/Disability Leave

**Definition:** Leave for medical condition or disability

**Commission Treatment:**
- No commission earned during leave
- Quota pro-rated for leave period
- Upon return: Ramp quota applied (see above)
- Extended medical leave may require termination discussion

**Short-term Disability:** <3 months
- Handled as unpaid leave

**Long-term Disability:** >3 months
- May transition to disability status vs. employment termination
- Consult HR/Legal for determination

### Parental Leave

**Definition:** Leave for birth/adoption of child

**Commission Treatment:**
- No commission earned during leave
- Quota pro-rated for leave period
- Up to 12 weeks FMLA-protected leave
- Upon return: Ramp quota applied

**Company Leave Policy:** See HR policies for additional benefits

### Military Leave

**Definition:** Military service or reserve/National Guard duty

**Commission Treatment:**
- Protected under USERRA (Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act)
- Upon return: Re-established to same or equivalent position
- No loss of seniority or commission rights
- Consult HR/Legal

---

## EXTENDED ABSENCE (>60 days)

**If LOA extends beyond 60 days:**
- Company may initiate severance discussion
- Rep may voluntarily terminate
- Temporary assignment of territory to another rep
- Upon return (if returning): Ramp quota and territory re-establishment

**If LOA extends beyond 90 days:**
- Employment status may be reconsidered
- Continuation of benefits evaluated
- Severance or return-to-work plan developed

---

## COMMISSION STATEMENT DURING LOA

**Statement Timing:**
- Regular statements still issued monthly
- Shows zero commission earned during leave
- Territory and quota pro-rated
- Any pending commission or adjustments noted

---

## DRAWS & GUARANTEES DURING LOA

**Draws:**
- Outstanding draw balance continues to accrue interest (if applicable)
- Draw advances not available during leave
- Repayment obligations resume upon return

**Guarantees:**
- Rep may be placed on guarantee during extended leave
- Amount: Based on pre-leave commission level
- Paid during leave, repayment upon return

---

## RETURN-TO-WORK PROCEDURES

**Manager Responsibility:**
1. Meet with rep 1 week before return
2. Review territory and quota changes
3. Explain ramp-back quota
4. Review any policy changes during leave
5. Set up meeting with compensation manager

**Compensation Manager Responsibility:**
1. Confirm return date
2. Update system for ramp quota
3. Restore territory assignments
4. Brief rep on any changes
5. Provide written confirmation

---

## DOCUMENTATION

**Required for LOA:**
- LOA request form (from HR)
- Approval from manager and HR
- Start date and expected return date
- Type of leave and expected duration
- Territory coverage plan
- Quota pro-ration calculation

**Upon Return:**
- Return-to-work memo
- Updated quota and territory assignments
- Ramp quota schedule (3 months)
- Updated commission statement

---

## SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

### Personal Leave (Family Care, Eldercare, etc.)

- Up to 10 days per year (unpaid)
- Pro-rated quota
- No commission during leave
- Same treatment as unpaid leave

### Sabbatical

- Not standard JAMF leave (require discussion with HR)
- If approved: Extended leave with return commitment
- Commission and territory treated as extended LOA
