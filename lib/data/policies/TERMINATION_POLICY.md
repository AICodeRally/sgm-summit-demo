# TERMINATION & SEVERANCE COMPENSATION POLICY

**Policy Code:** SCP-004
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** APPROVED
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## PURPOSE

Define commission treatment upon employment termination (voluntary or involuntary).

---

## EARNED VS. UNEARNED COMMISSION

**Earned Commission:**
- Commission on deals closed before termination date
- Rep is entitled to this commission
- Paid via final paycheck within state law timeframes

**Unearned Commission:**
- Commission on deals not yet closed at termination
- Rep forfeits this (except in specific cases below)
- Not paid to rep

---

## VOLUNTARY TERMINATION

**Rep Initiated Resignation:**

1. **Commission through termination date:** PAID (all deals closed by termination date)
2. **Unearned commission in pipeline:** NOT PAID
3. **Draws outstanding:** Deducted from final payment
4. **Final payment timing:** Per state law (CA: within 72 hours)

**Notice Period:** Standard notice applies (typically 2 weeks)
- Commission earned during notice period is paid
- Commission payable through actual last day

---

## INVOLUNTARY TERMINATION

**Company-Initiated Termination (Layoff, Performance, etc.):**

1. **Commission through termination date:** PAID
2. **Severance (if applicable):** Per severance agreement
3. **Deals in final stage (within 30 days of close):** Company discretion
   - If deal closes within 30 days: May be paid to rep or assigned to manager
   - Documented in severance agreement
4. **Draws outstanding:** Deducted from final payment
5. **Final payment timing:** IMMEDIATELY (involuntary termination)

**Severance Consideration:** May include commission continuation, see severance agreement

---

## DEATH OR DISABILITY

**If rep dies or becomes permanently disabled:**

1. **Commission through date of death/disability:** Paid to estate or rep (if disability)
2. **In-progress deals:**
   - Up to 60 days: Paid if customer-signed
   - After 60 days: Company discretion
3. **Draws:** Deducted from final payment
4. **Final payment:** Expedited, within 5 business days

---

## SPECIAL SITUATIONS

### Terminated for Cause (Fraud, Misconduct, etc.)

- Commission earned and paid ONLY for deals properly credited
- Any commissions obtained via fraud or misconduct are subject to clawback
- Investigation may delay final payment pending determination

### Leave of Absence Followed by Termination

- LOA period: Handled per Leave of Absence Policy
- Upon termination after LOA: Normal termination rules apply
- Commissions earned during active work periods paid

### Retirement

- Treated as voluntary termination
- All earned commission paid
- Unearned commission not paid (unless special arrangement)

---

## FINAL PAYMENT CALCULATION

**Final Check Includes:**
1. Earned commission through termination date
2. Unpaid regular compensation (salary, bonus, etc.)
3. Accrued paid time off (per company policy)
4. Minus: Any draws/advances outstanding
5. Minus: Any clawback amounts owed

**Payment Processing:**
- Calculated by Finance + Payroll
- Reviewed by Compensation Manager for accuracy
- Taxes withheld per applicable law
- Issued per state wage law timing requirements

---

## STATE WAGE LAW COMPLIANCE

**Timing by State:**
- **California:** Immediately for involuntary; within 72 hours for voluntary
- **Texas:** Within 5 business days
- **New York:** On regular payday
- **Other states:** Per applicable law

**Documentation:**
- Final pay statement provided
- Commission calculation documented
- Draws reconciliation shown
- Provided to rep at time of payment

---

## MULTI-YEAR DEAL TREATMENT

**If rep terminated with multi-year deals in progress:**

**Option 1 - Full Payment:** Pay commission on multi-year deal in full
**Option 2 - Amortized Payment:** Pay commission over contract term
**Option 3 - No Payment:** If deal not yet signed by termination date

**Determination:** Company discretion, documented in severance agreement

---

## CLAWBACK UPON TERMINATION

If rep owes clawback (due to customer cancellation or fraud):
- Clawback amount deducted from final payment
- If final payment insufficient, rep responsible for balance
- Follow Clawback Policy procedures

---

## DOCUMENTATION

**Required For Each Termination:**
- Termination letter (date, reason)
- Final commission calculation
- Draw reconciliation
- Clawback determination (if any)
- Final pay statement
- Rep acknowledgment of amounts
