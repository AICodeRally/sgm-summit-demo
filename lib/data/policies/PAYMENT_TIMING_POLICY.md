# PAYMENT TIMING POLICY

**Policy Code:** SCP-003
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** APPROVED
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## PURPOSE

Define when commission is earned, when it is paid, and payment procedures.

---

## COMMISSION EARNED DATE

Commission is **earned** when:
- Deal is closed (signed contract by customer) AND
- Deal is recorded in Salesforce with proper account assignment AND
- Quota credit is applied to rep's account

**Earned Date = Contract Signature Date** (not when booked in system)

---

## PAYMENT TIMING

### Monthly Commission Payments

**Payment Schedule:**
- Commission earned in Month 1 → Paid on 20th of following month
- Example: January deals → Paid February 20
- Payment method: Direct deposit or company check

**Payment Confirmation:**
- Reps receive electronic commission statement
- Direct deposit confirmation via banking system
- Payment notice via email

### Special Payment Situations

**Draws/Advances:**
- Available per Draw Policy
- Repayment at year-end or upon termination
- Offset against regular commission

**Year-End True-Up:**
- Performed in January for prior calendar year
- Reconciles all commissions earned in prior year
- Adjusts for any calculation corrections
- Issues payment or deducts overpayment

---

## COMMISSION STATEMENTS

**Distribution Timing:**
- Issued by 15th of each month (for prior month's earnings)
- Includes calculation detail
- Provided to rep and manager

**Statement Contents:**
- Deals closed in period
- Metric achievement
- Commission earned
- YTD totals
- Any adjustments or SPIFs
- Contact info for questions

---

## TERMINATION PAYMENTS

**Upon Termination:**
- All commission through last day of employment paid
- Draws reconciled (any balance due paid or deducted from final pay)
- Timing per state law (California: within 72 hours for voluntary, immediately for involuntary)
- See Termination Policy for details

---

## TIMING FOR SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

**Calculation Corrections:**
- If error discovered: Corrected in next month's statement
- If significant (<$1K): Paid in regular monthly payment
- If significant (>$1K): May be paid as separate check within 5 business days

**Dispute Resolution:**
- During dispute, regular commission paid on time
- Disputed amount held pending resolution
- Upon resolution, difference paid within 5 business days

**Clawback/Recovery:**
- Recovered via payroll deduction from future commission
- Or lump sum if rep has agreed to repayment terms
- Timeline per Clawback Policy

---

## COMPENSATION COMMITTEE DECISIONS

**Effective Date of Policy Changes:**
- New policy changes effective on stated date
- Commission for period is calculated under applicable policy
- Example: Mid-month policy change → Calculated per actual policy days

---

## YEAR-END ACCRUAL

**December Commission:**
- Earned in December → Paid January 20
- Year-end bonus (if applicable) → Paid January 31
- Year-end reconciliation → Completed by January 31
