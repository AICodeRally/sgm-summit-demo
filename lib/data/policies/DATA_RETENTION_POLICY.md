# DATA RETENTION & RECORD-KEEPING POLICY

**Policy Code:** SCP-009
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** APPROVED
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## PURPOSE

Establish standards for retaining compensation-related records to comply with legal requirements and support audits and disputes.

---

## RETENTION PERIODS BY RECORD TYPE

| Record Type | Retention Period | Notes |
|-------------|-----------------|-------|
| Commission statements | 7 years | Legal requirement (wage law) |
| Compensation agreements | 10 years | Verify terms, disputes, litigation |
| Quota documentation | 7 years | May support disputes, appeals |
| Territory assignments | 5 years | Minimum; 7 if dispute pending |
| Plan documents | 10 years | Maintain version history |
| Commission calculations | 7 years | Support reconciliation, audits |
| Approval documents | 7 years | SPIF, exception, clawback approvals |
| Exception requests | 7 years | Record of decisions made |
| Appeals & disputes | 7 years minimum | Indefinite if litigation pending |
| CRB decision logs | 10 years | Precedent and consistency tracking |
| Draws & guarantees | 7 years | Verify repayment/reconciliation |
| Clawback notices | 10 years | Legal protection, disputes |
| Termination letters | 7 years | Final commission, severance |
| Tax documentation | 7 years | IRS requirement (1099, W-2) |
| Audit work papers | 7 years | Support historical review |

## STORAGE REQUIREMENTS

### Digital Records

**Location:** Secure shared drive or document management system
- Access: Role-based access controls
- Backup: Daily backup, offsite copy
- Encryption: Data encrypted at rest and in transit
- Retention: Automatic deletion upon retention period expiration (with legal hold exceptions)

### Paper Records

**Location:** Locked file cabinet or secure storage
- Access: Limited to authorized personnel only
- Climate: Temperature/humidity controlled
- Security: Fire suppression, theft prevention
- Retention: Shredded upon retention period expiration

### HR Records (Separate)

**Maintained by:** Human Resources Department
- Retention: Per HR policy (typically 3-7 years)
- Integration: Cross-referenced with compensation records

---

## RECORD SECURITY & ACCESS

**Who Can Access Compensation Records:**

- **Rep:** Own compensation records only
- **Manager:** Team members' records only
- **Sales Operations:** All current operational records
- **Finance:** Commission data for reconciliation
- **Legal:** As requested for compliance/litigation
- **Audit:** Annual audit access to selected records
- **Executive (CSO, CFO, VP Comp):** Full access

**Access Log:**
- All access logged and tracked
- Quarterly review of access logs
- Suspicious access reported to Security

---

## LEGAL HOLDS

**If litigation or investigation pending:**

- **Litigation Hold Notice:** Issued by Legal
- **Scope:** Specifies records to be retained beyond normal period
- **Duration:** Until lawsuit resolved or investigation complete
- **Notification:** All personnel notified that records are on hold
- **Compliance:** All policies to retain applicable records must be followed

**Records Subject to Hold:**
- Cannot be deleted
- Cannot be modified
- Must be preserved in original form
- May be requested for discovery

---

## AUDIT & COMPLIANCE

### Annual Audit

**Internal or external auditors** have access to:
- Commission calculation samples (typically 10%)
- SPIF approvals and payments
- Exception request approvals
- Clawback notices and recovery
- Quota documentation
- Territory assignments

**Auditors may request:**
- Full files for specific reps or periods
- Reconciliation analysis (payroll vs commission system)
- Control testing (segregation of duties, approvals)
- Compliance verification (state laws, tax withholding)

### Retention During Audit

- All records requested in audit must be retained
- Even if normal retention period has passed
- Until audit is complete and findings resolved

---

## DISPOSAL PROCEDURES

### Digital Records

**Upon Retention Expiration:**
- Move to archive (do not delete)
- After 1-year archive period → Secure deletion
- Secure deletion methods: Data destruction software or certified shredding service
- Confirmation of deletion documented

### Paper Records

**Upon Retention Expiration:**
- Move to storage (not immediately destroyed)
- After 1-year period → Shred or certified destruction
- Destruction must be certified (certificate obtained and filed)
- Chain of custody maintained

---

## EXCEPTIONS TO RETENTION

**Records retained beyond standard period:**

1. **Pending Litigation** - Until case resolved
2. **Pending Dispute** - Until rep appeals exhausted
3. **Pending Investigation** - Until investigation complete
4. **Special Request** - By rep or legal counsel
5. **Executive Decision** - If strategic value or precedent

---

## BACKUP & DISASTER RECOVERY

### Digital Backup

**Frequency:** Daily automated backup
- Primary: On-site encrypted drive
- Secondary: Off-site cloud backup
- Rotation: Oldest backup deleted after retention period
- Testing: Quarterly backup restoration testing

**Disaster Recovery Plan:**
- If primary system failure: Restore from backup within 24 hours
- If data loss: Minimum 30-day recovery window (backups retained 30 days)
- Testing: Annual disaster recovery drill

---

## CROSS-FUNCTIONAL COORDINATION

**Compensation + Finance:**
- Commission data reconciled monthly
- Supporting documents maintained by both departments
- Shared retention requirements (7 years minimum)

**Compensation + HR:**
- HR maintains employment records (separate)
- Cross-referenced with compensation records
- Disputes may require both sets of records

**Compensation + Legal:**
- Legal may request records for compliance review
- Maintains separate legal holds
- Coordinates retention of litigation-related documents

---

## RETENTION POLICY VIOLATION

**If records destroyed without authorization:**
- Immediate investigation
- Disciplinary action for responsible party
- Possible legal liability (especially if litigation-related)
- Notification to affected parties
- Restoration from backup if available

---

## POLICY REVIEW

**Retention periods reviewed annually:**
- Changes in legal requirements assessed
- Storage capacity evaluated
- Technology changes considered
- Compliance effectiveness verified
- Policy updated as needed

---

## INDIVIDUAL RECORD REQUESTS

**Rep can request copy of own records:**
- Submit written request
- Provided within 10 business days
- Costs covered by company (no charge to rep)
- Formats: Electronic or paper copy available

**Third-party requests (legal, regulatory):**
- Handled by Legal Counsel
- Subpoena compliance procedures followed
- Records provided per legal requirements
