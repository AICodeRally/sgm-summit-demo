# Plans / Remediation

## Purpose
Track governance gaps and remediation steps tied to a plan.

## When to use
Use this page when a plan has open gaps or policy issues.

## How to use
1. Review the gap list and severity.
2. Assign owners and target dates.
3. Mark items complete as remediation progresses.

## Key data
Gap severity, remediation status, owners, and due dates.
