# Analytics

## Purpose
Provide dashboards for governance health, risk posture, and operational performance.

## When to use
Use this page for executive reviews, compliance tracking, and trend analysis.

## How to use
1. Select the analytics view (risk, coverage, approvals).
2. Filter by time period or business unit.
3. Use insights to prioritize remediation or policy updates.

## Key data
Coverage percentages, gap counts, SLA compliance, and risk scores.
