# Governance Matrix

## Purpose
Show how policies map to plan sections, committees, and compliance requirements.

## When to use
Use this page to assess coverage gaps, identify missing approvals, or verify governance alignment.

## How to use
1. Review the matrix to see policy-to-section coverage.
2. Filter by policy category or committee ownership.
3. Drill into gaps to open related policies or documents.

## Key data
Policy codes, mapped sections, approval tiers, and coverage status.
