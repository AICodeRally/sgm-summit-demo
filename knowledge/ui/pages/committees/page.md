# Committees

## Purpose
Coordinate committee membership, meetings, and decision tracking for SGCC and CRB.

## When to use
Use this page to review committee rosters, schedule meetings, and track decisions.

## How to use
1. Open a committee to review members and upcoming meetings.
2. Review recent decisions and approvals.
3. Link decisions to related plans or policies.

## Key data
Committee membership, meeting cadence, decision counts, and approval status.
