# Oversee

## Purpose
Provide leadership visibility into committee activity, audit trails, and governance health.

## When to use
Use this page for executive reviews, committee oversight, compliance monitoring, and risk posture checks.

## How to use
1. Open Committee Management to review SGCC/CRB membership and decisions.
2. Use Audit Timeline to trace governance events.
3. Check Governance Analytics for KPIs and trends.
4. Use Oversight Tools for decisions log, compliance monitor, and risk assessment.

## Key data
Committee counts, meeting cadence, decision volume, and compliance score.
